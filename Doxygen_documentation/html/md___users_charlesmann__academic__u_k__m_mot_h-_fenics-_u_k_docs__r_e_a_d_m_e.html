  • Sample top navigation.
  • Sample author sidebar with social links.
  • Sample footer links.
  • Paginated home page.
  • Archive pages for posts grouped by year, category, and tag.
  • Sample about page.
  • Sample 404 page.
  • Site wide search.

Replace sample content with your own and configure as necessary.
